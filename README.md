The premise of this is as follows. Spotify and other music categorization apps suck donkey nuts–they're absolutely terrible. What do you mean Hotel California is the same genre as Jutes? Nah.

Thus, I decided to make my own categorizer. 

You can search by genre, subgenre, era, language, energy, and probably some more stuff that I added after writing this README.

The search bar will eventually be able to filter through by songs and act as a second side bar.

Maybe I'll add pages for each song that you visit that'll give an overview of its various subgenres and other tags, as well as similar songs, or maybe it'll just direct you to its spotify or something, idk yet.

In addition, the database (that doesn't exist yet) will be hand-crafted by yours truly, becuase I refuse to use AI for database crafting.
